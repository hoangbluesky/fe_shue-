Details & Benefits

Bring a sense of pure calm by unlocking the herbal notes of our vegan De-Stress Candle.


Please Note: This product cannot be shipped outside of the UK.

Richly aromatic, our hand-poured soy wax contains two 10ml bottles of pure essential oils thatwill burn for over 40 hoursfor a delicate aroma reaching every corner of your home.

The De-Stress islight and herbaceous, ablend created for mild cases of disquiet, when the pressures of life are getting a little too much and your focus, sleep and relationships are all being put to the test. Crafted with Petitgrain for clarity, Chamomile to inspire a sense of calm, and Frankincense the latter known as a filing cabinet for the brain to boost concentration.

Plus, this candle is encased in a premium ceramic jar you can re-use or recycle.

How to use

  1. 1

    Take the candle out of the box and be sure to remove the coloured paper from the top of the candle.

  2. 2
    Light the candle and let the therapeutic aromas fill your home and surround your senses.
  3. 3
    For the most even burn, be sure to extinguish at an even level of fully pooled oil. Once the candle is fully burned, the ceramic holder is perfect to reuse as a stylish pot.

Sensible Cautions

Warning: May cause an allergic skin reaction. Harmful to awuatic life with long lasting effects. Avoid release to the environment. IF ON SKIN: Wash with plenty of soap and water. If skin irritation or rash occurs: Get medical advice/attention. Dispose of contents/container in accordance with local regulations. Contains Linalool, Eucalyptol, Linalyl Acetate, alpha-Pinene. May produce an allergic reaction. Burn for a maximum of 4 hours at a time.
